Icream Store

Order Management

iCream, the ice cream store has launched a new brand of ice-creams that has become a smashing success with their customers. There has been a surge in the number of customers frequenting their stores and it has become difficult to handle this demand. Consequently, they have been losing out on potential customers who grow tired of waiting for their orders to be taken and leave.

To overcome this problem iCream has decided to move their ordering experience to the next level with an easy to use and feasible dashboard where Managers can view, organize, handle and finalize customersâ€™ orders using mobile phone.

Goal

Develop an intuitive mobile dashboard for iCream, empowering efficient organization and real-time management of customer orders. This innovative tool will streamline operations and enhance order tracking, ensuring timely and delightful customer experiences.
